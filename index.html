About One Piece

One Piece is a Japanese anime and manga series following Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew as they explore the Grand Line in search of the ultimate treasure: the One Piece.

Monkey D. Luffy

Captain of the Straw Hat Pirates. A rubber man who dreams of becoming the Pirate King.

Roronoa Zoro

The swordsman of the crew who wields three swords and aims to be the world’s greatest.

Nami

The navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates with a love for maps and treasure.
